Munnar, Kerala


Munnar is a peaceful hill station nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala. Famous for its endless tea plantations, cool weather, and misty landscapes, it attracts nature lovers and honeymooners from all over the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eravikulam National Park – Known for the Nilgiri Tahr and lush grasslands.
  2. Mattupetty Dam – A place to enjoy boating and scenic views.
  3. Kundala Lake – Offers pedal boat rides and cherry blossoms in winter.
  4. Top Station – Famous for breathtaking mountain views.
  5. Echo Point – Where your voice bounces back through the hills.

Must-Try Foods

  • Kerala Sadya – A lavish vegetarian meal served on a banana leaf.
  • Appam with Vegetable Stew – Soft rice pancakes with creamy coconut curry.
  • Fish Pollichathu – Fish marinated and cooked in banana leaf wraps.
  • Malabar Parotta – A flaky bread served with spicy meat curry.
  • Pazham Pori – Crispy banana fritters, a perfect tea-time snack.
