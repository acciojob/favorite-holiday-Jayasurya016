Munnar


Munnar is a serene hill station in Kerala, known for its lush green tea gardens, misty hills, and pleasant climate throughout the year.

  1. Tea Gardens - Vast, scenic plantations spread across hills.
  2. Eravikulam National Park - Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
  3. Mattupetty Dam - A peaceful spot for boating and picnics.
  4. Echo Point - Known for its natural echo phenomenon.
  5. Attukal Waterfalls - A mesmerizing cascade in the forest.
  • Appam with Stew - A light and flavorful dish.
  • Kerala Sadya - A traditional feast served on banana leaves.
  • Malabar Parotta with spicy curry.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry - A healthy breakfast combo.
  • Filter Coffee - Aromatic and energizing!
