Munnar, Kerala


Munnar is a serene hill station located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. Known for its lush green tea plantations, misty mountains, and cool climate, Munnar offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eravikulam National Park – Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
  2. Mattupetty Dam – A popular picnic spot surrounded by scenic hills.
  3. Tea Museum – Learn about the rich history of tea plantations in Munnar.
  4. Top Station – Offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the Western Ghats.
  5. Echo Point – A fun spot where your voice bounces back from the hills!

Must-Try Foods

  • Kerala Sadya – A traditional feast served on a banana leaf.
  • Appam with Stew – Soft hoppers paired with a fragrant coconut curry.
  • Malabar Parotta – Flaky bread served with spicy meat curry.
  • Fish Moilee – A deliciously mild coconut fish curry.
  • Pazham Pori – Sweet banana fritters that are perfect with tea!
