Munnar, Kerala

Munnar is a breathtaking hill station in Kerala, India, known for its sprawling tea plantations, misty mountains, and serene lakes. Often called the "Kashmir of South India," Munnar offers cool weather, lush greenery, and a peaceful escape into nature.

Top 5 Attractions in Munnar

  1. Eravikulam National Park - Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and stunning views of rolling hills
  2. Tea Gardens - Vast, picturesque plantations where you can learn about tea production
  3. Mattupetty Dam - A scenic spot perfect for boating and spotting wildlife
  4. Echo Point - Famous for its natural echo phenomenon and beautiful lake
  5. Top Station - Offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats and neighboring Tamil Nadu

Must-Try Foods in Munnar

  • Kerala Sadya - A traditional vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf
  • Appam with Stew - Soft rice pancakes served with coconut milk-based vegetable or meat stew
  • Malabar Parotta with Beef Curry - Flaky layered bread with spicy beef preparation
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry - Steamed rice cakes with black chickpea curry
  • Fresh Tea - Munnar's world-famous tea, best enjoyed at a local plantation
