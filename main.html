Munnar


Munnar is a beautiful hill station in Kerala, India, known for its lush green tea gardens, misty mountains, and serene atmosphere. It's a perfect retreat for nature lovers and peace seekers.

  1. Tea Gardens - Explore the sprawling green plantations that make Munnar so iconic.
  2. Eravikulam National Park - Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and stunning landscapes.
  3. Mattupetty Dam - A picturesque spot ideal for boating and sightseeing.
  4. Top Station - Offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats and Tamil Nadu valley.
  5. Echo Point - Famous for its natural echo phenomenon and scenic beauty.
