Munnar, Kerala


Munnar is a breathtaking hill station located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. Known for its lush green tea plantations, misty mountains, and pleasant climate, it is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers.

Top 5 Attractions in Munnar

  1. Tea Gardens – Experience the beauty of endless green landscapes.
  2. Eravikulam National Park – Home to the rare Nilgiri Tahr.
  3. Mattupetty Dam – A scenic spot for boating and sightseeing.
  4. Echo Point – Famous for its natural echo phenomenon.
  5. Top Station – Offers breathtaking panoramic views of the valley.

Must-Try Foods in Munnar

  • Kerala Sadya – A traditional vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf.
  • Appam with Stew – Soft hoppers paired with a rich coconut curry.
  • Malabar Parotta – Flaky bread best enjoyed with beef curry.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry – A steamed rice cake with black chickpea curry.
  • Cardamom Tea – A refreshing local specialty from Munnar’s plantations.
